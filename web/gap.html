Gap Analysis

Have one framework, pursuing another?

Pick a framework you already meet and one you are working toward. Kyora IQ shows which target controls your existing controls already cover through a mapping, and which are gaps with no mapping yet.

This is a planning aid, not an audit. "Covered" means a Kyora IQ mapping links the target control to one you already have, not that the control is implemented in your environment. A gap may be a real gap, or simply a mapping no one has authored yet.
